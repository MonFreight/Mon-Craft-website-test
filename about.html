About Us

Where Craft Meets Construction

A Brisbane installation studio built on one belief: the final millimetre matters as much as the first.

Our Story

Installation Is All We Do

Mon Craft was founded on a simple observation: beautifully made joinery is too often let down at the final step — rushed fitting, poor scribing, defect call-backs.

So we built a company devoted entirely to that final step. We don't supply. We don't manufacture. Installation is our entire craft — completing what other joinery companies make. Our installers think like cabinetmakers and work like builders, treating every panel as if it were furniture.

Today we install everything from single bespoke kitchens to complete multi-residential packages across South East Queensland, and our repeat-client rate tells the story.
